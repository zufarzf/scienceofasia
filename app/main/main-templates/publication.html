Applicable to Submission from September 1, 2021 onwards.
ScienceAsia publishes broad spectrum of papers in science and mathematics. Currently, the publication charge is USD 500 per research/review article (or USD 350 for a short report), the rate implemented as of January 1, 2018. Since the journal is supported by the National Research Council of Thailand, the publication fee for subsidized Thai corresponding authors or foreign corresponding authors working in Thai universities or institutions for at least 5 years is 10,000 Baht (for a research/review article) or 7,000 Baht (for a short report). The corresponding authors with life-time membership of the Science Society of Thailand (proof of membership is required) are eligible for a 20% discount (8,000 Baht for a research/review article or 5,600 Baht for a short report). The publication fee excludes the cost of printing graphics or figures in full color for the hard copy version (optional) which will be an additional USD 1,000 (foreign corresponding authors) or 15,000 Baht (Thai corresponding authors) per paper. In all cases, payments must be settled soon after the paper is accepted.
